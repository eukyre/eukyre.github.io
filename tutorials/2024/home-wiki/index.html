Whether you’re new to SPT or a seasoned modder, this wiki aims to be your central hub for information.SPT allows you to experience Escape From Tarkov offline, in a single-player environment complete with progression, quests, AI Scavs, and AI PMCs. It uses server emulation and client modifications to create a highly customizable, personal Tarkov experience, completely separate from the live online game.

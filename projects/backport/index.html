Content Backport

A backport of all 1.0 EFT content to SPT 4.0 & 4.1.

What’s Included?

Mod Description

This mod adds virtually EVERY new item, clothing, hideout customization, voice line, and character head from the 1.0 release. If you’ve been curious what the live version added, it’s all here.

Content Added:

  • All new weapons, attachments, and gear
  • Character customization (heads, clothing, voices)
  • Hideout upgrades and decorations
  • Barter items, food, and medical supplies
  • New ending environments + Miami environment
  • Custom WTT Achievements for unlocking some of the exclusive items

What’s Different from 1.0?

We couldn’t backport everything; here’s what’s missing:

  • Live Achievements + Quest content. No main story.
  • Terminal, and any reworked maps (labs, interchange, ect)

What we did rebuild:

All automatic weapons were reconstructed from scratch to work properly in SPT. Both myself and teammate Eco underwent this project; he did most of the work, I did the sounds :D

How to Unlock Content

Option 1: Achievements - We’ve added achievable unlock conditions for Twitch drops, preorder content, and other exclusive items. Earn them naturally as you play. You can see these achievements in your achievements tab, as well as the unlock conditions for each.

Option 2: Cheat Codes - Use Commando commands to unlock everything instantly. Open a chat with “Commando” in your friends list, type help to Commando to see all available commands. If there is no Commando in your friends list, you more than likely have Fika installed disabling Commando from being used. These achievements are HIDDEN, the ONLY way to see what they do is to use them. FAFO

You can find a comprehensive list of commands on the WTT Wiki, here.

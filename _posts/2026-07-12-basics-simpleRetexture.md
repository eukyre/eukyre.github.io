<p>This tutorial will demonstrate how to re-texture a simple item in Single Player Tarkov. For simplicity's sake, we will be using
a barter item with no modslots or type-specific scripts.</p>

**THIS TUTORIAL WILL NOT COVER RIPPING AN ITEM FROM EFT, YOU CAN FIND A GUIDE ON THAT [HERE].**

### For this tutorial, you will need:

- [Adobe Substance Painter 3D](https://www.adobe.com/products/substance3d/apps/painter.html)
- [AssetStudioModGUI v0.16](https://github.com/Perfare/AssetStudio/releases)
- [Blender v4.3 or below](https://www.blender.org/download/previous-versions/)
- [Escape From Tarkov (obviously)](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3932890/Escape_from_Tarkov/)
- [GameImageUtil](https://github.com/Scobalula/GameImageUtil/releases)
- [WTT Unity 2022 SDK](https://github.com/WelcomeToTarkov/WTT-SDK-Public/releases)
- An IDE:
  - [JetBrains Rider](https://www.jetbrains.com/rider/download/?section=windows) **(Recommended)**
  - [Visual Studio](https://visualstudio.microsoft.com/)
- patience (optional)
